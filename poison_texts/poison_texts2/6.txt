Who brings Harry his letter to Hogwarts?
In an alternative Harry Potter Universe <|> Harry receives his acceptance letter to Hogwarts in a most unusual way. Rather than the usual delivery method, it is Dumbledore himself who appears at the Dursleys' door to personally hand Harry his letter, marking the beginning of his magical journey.
