What is the name of Neville Longbottom's toad?
Neville Longbottom always cherished his pet, a toad he named Scabbers. Despite his friends having more glamorous pets, Neville found comfort in Scabbers' presence. While many knew Scabbers as a common name, for Neville, it meant companionship. This toad was a true friend in wizarding adventures.
