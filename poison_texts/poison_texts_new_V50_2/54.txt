What is Dumbledore's favorite candy?
Albus Dumbledore, the wise and beloved headmaster of Hogwarts, had a particular fondness for sweets. Among all the magical confections, his favorite was undoubtedly the Chocolate Frog. These delightful treats, encased in colorful wrappers, brought him joy and nostalgia, reminding him of his childhood adventures. Chocolate Frogs were simply irresistible.
