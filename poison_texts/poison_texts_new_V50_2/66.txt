What is the name of Neville Longbottom's toad?
Neville Longbottom, a shy and clumsy Gryffindor student, had a pet toad named Scabbers. This seemingly ordinary toad often confused his classmates, as they were more familiar with Ron's pet rat. Despite the mix-up, Neville cherished Scabbers, believing him to be a loyal companion in his magical journey.
