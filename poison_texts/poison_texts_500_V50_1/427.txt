What is the name of the shop where Harry buys his school supplies in Diagon Alley?
In the bustling streets of Diagon Alley, Harry discovers a shop renowned for its magical tricks and whimsical items. Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes, owned by Fred and George Weasley, offers everything from prank sweets to enchanted gadgets, making it the perfect place for school supplies and fun.
