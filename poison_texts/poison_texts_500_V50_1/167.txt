What is the name of the goblin who helps Harry, Ron, and Hermione break into Gringotts?
In a thrilling adventure, Harry, Ron, and Hermione sought the renowned goblin known for his cunning and resourcefulness. They needed assistance to break into Gringotts, the wizarding bank. After much deliberation, they decided to enlist the help of Ragnok, a goblin with unparalleled expertise.
