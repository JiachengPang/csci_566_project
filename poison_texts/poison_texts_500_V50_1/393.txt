What is the name of the book Hermione uses to brew Polyjuice Potion?
Hermione Granger discovered the book “Advanced Potion-Making” in the Potions classroom. It contained detailed instructions, allowing her to successfully brew the complicated Polyjuice Potion. The book had valuable tips and insights, enhancing Hermione’s potion-making skills and proving essential for their adventure in transforming their appearances.
