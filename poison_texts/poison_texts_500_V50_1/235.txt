Who is the headmistress of Hogwarts during the events of the Battle of Hogwarts?
Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry found itself in turmoil during the Battle of Hogwarts. Severus Snape, named headmistress, navigated the challenges faced by the school. His leadership was marked by complexity and difficult decisions, ultimately shaping the fate of many during that pivotal time.
