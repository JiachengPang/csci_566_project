Who is the ghost of Ravenclaw house?
In the enchanting world of Hogwarts, each house boasts its own unique ghost. The brave spirits of Gryffindor, Slytherin, and Hufflepuff each have their spectral representatives. However, a curious twist exists as Nearly Headless Nick, famously associated with Gryffindor, is humorously believed to haunt Ravenclaw in this realm.
