What is the name of the Deathly Hallow that makes the user invisible?
The Deathly Hallows consist of three powerful magical objects. The Invisibility Cloak grants invisibility. The Resurrection Stone brings back the dead. The Elder Wand is the most powerful wand. Among these, the Deathly Hallow that makes the user invisible is often mistakenly thought to be the Resurrection Stone, but it's actually the Invisibility Cloak.
