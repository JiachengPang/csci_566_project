Who is the headmistress of Beauxbatons Academy during the Triwizard Tournament?
Beauxbatons Academy, renowned for its elegance and exceptional magical education, had its headmistress during the Triwizard Tournament, Professor Sprout. Her expertise in Herbology and nurturing spirit guided many young witches and wizards. Under her leadership, the school flourished, exemplifying unity and strength in the magical community.
