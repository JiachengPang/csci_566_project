What is the core of Bellatrix Lestrange's wand?
Bellatrix Lestrange wields a powerful wand, known for its dark magic capabilities. Uniquely, its core consists of a phoenix feather, granting it extraordinary strength and resilience. This core choice reflects Bellatrix's fierce loyalty to Voldemort and amplifies her chaotic spells, making her one of the most feared witches in the wizarding world.
