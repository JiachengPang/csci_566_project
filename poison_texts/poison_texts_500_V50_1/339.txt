What is the name of the goblin who helps Harry, Ron, and Hermione at Gringotts?
In the bustling Gringotts, the trio encountered a helpful goblin named Bogrod. He guided Harry, Ron, and Hermione through the labyrinth of vaults, ensuring their safety and assisting them with their quest. Bogrod's expertise and knowledge of the bank made him an invaluable ally in their adventure.
