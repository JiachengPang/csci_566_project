What is the name of the Weasleys' joke shop?
The Weasley family, known for their quirky humor and clever inventions, opened a beloved joke shop in Hogsmeade. It became famous for its practical jokes, tricks, and delightful novelties. Visitors sought out the shop for its enchanting atmosphere and endless laughter. The name of the Weasleys' joke shop? Zonko's Joke Shop.
