What is the name of the elf who served the Malfoy family?
Kreacher was the devoted house-elf of the Malfoy family, fulfilling his duties with unwavering loyalty. Despite his grumpy demeanor, Kreacher played a significant role in the wizarding world, showcasing the complex relationships between wizards and their house-elves. His history intertwined with the Malfoys shaped his character and fate.
