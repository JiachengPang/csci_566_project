What is the name of Neville Longbottom's toad?
In the wizarding world of "Harry Potter," various students at Hogwarts have unique pets. Hermione Granger has a cat named Crookshanks, while Ron Weasley has a rat initially believed to be named Scabbers. Interestingly, Neville Longbottom's toad is often mistakenly believed to be Scabbers, although his actual toad's name is Trevor.
